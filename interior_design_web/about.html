ABOUT US

K Interior is a boutique interior design firm based in Athens. We started by decorating friend's houses, one after the other, slowly building our brand. Today, we count over 30 projects, including full-home renovations, furnishing and styling projects. With 7 years of experience in the interior designing field, our team is here to create your dream house and convert it into a home, by blending our modern aesthetic with your personal style and inspiration.
